Standard Curriculum

Kinder (3-6) Years

Play-based curriculum fostering creativity, social skills, early literacy, numeracy, motor development, exploration, problem-solving, and a love for learning.

Elementary School

The elementary school curriculum typically includes reading, writing, mathematics, science, social studies, arts, physical education, technology, and social-emotional learning.

Middle (10-16) Years

The middle years curriculum covers language arts, math, science, social studies, arts, physical education, technology, critical thinking, and personal development.
